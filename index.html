Our Story

From Ghana to the World – With Heart, Hustle, and Purpose

My name is Alberta Ocran, and I founded AO Pro Solutions. Born in Ghana, I was conceived during my parents' time in Nigeria, then joined them in Italy in 1987 as a little girl. There, I grew up, studied, and began my journey helping people—starting with reading and translating documents for my parents and the broader African community.

That passion grew into a profession: I worked with Citifinancial Bank, partnered with mortgage brokers, and served as a vital bridge between Italian legal systems and the Ghanaian community. After relocating to the UK, I pursued studies in dentistry, worked as a dental nurse, and now work in a cancer hospital—a role that deeply aligns with my calling to serve others.

❤️ Founder's Message

"Life is a gift—let's help you enjoy it fully."

Working in a cancer hospital reminds me daily how short and precious life is. Too often, I see people from my Ghanaian community—and immigrant communities as a whole—working endlessly and sacrificing so much but rarely taking the time to rest, explore, or celebrate life.

That's why AO Pro Solutions isn't just about paperwork. It's about helping you experience more. A dream inspired our travel services: to help hardworking people make beautiful memories with loved ones and to see the world they've helped build.

This business is a work in progress, but one thing is sure: AO Pro Solutions is here to stay and grow with you.

With love,
Alberta Ocran
